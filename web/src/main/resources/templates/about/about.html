168 Hours

This service can help you with balancing your time spending. Just prepare your hierarchy of activities in categories section and you are good to go.

Add days, breaking them into time segments, and set category for each segment depending on what you spend it on.

This will allow you to look at your overall spending per category per week (or any other time period) and adjust you activity priorities.

Based on “168 Hours: You Have More Time Than You Think” by Laura Vanderkam
